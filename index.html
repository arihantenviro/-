About us

✓ Arihant Enviro Services (AES) was established in 2016 having head office at Udaipur(Raj.).

✓ We are offering our services to all categories of industries/projects/activities/sectors for obtaining various Environmental Permissions/Consents/ NOC/Authorization from regulatory bodies i.e. MoEFCC, State Pollution Control Board(RSPCB etc.), CPCB, CGWA/CGWB, Forest Deptt., Mining Deptt. etc.

✓ We assure our best services for timely preparation & submission of compliance reports & other mendatory reports/returns, Water & wastewater management, Complying with various Environmental Laws, Pollution Management, Environmental Monitoring & testing by approved labs, Hazardous Waste(i.e. Used Oil, Waste Oil, Bio-Medical, E-Waste, Lead-Acid Batteries etc.) Management, Solid Waste Management etc.

✓ We assure quality work and best solutions to our customers.
